Tier + Weather = the conditions. They set how hard the run is and how you should fight (bonus, penalty, the damage type to exploit) — they do not pick your enemies.

Faction = the room you actually landed in. Each run's rooms are semi-random. Read the enemy names in your overview, then pick that faction on the left to see how to beat it.
